Our Story

Milestone Technologies is a dynamic organization Headquartered in Chennai, India, with its primary development center strategically situated in Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Milestone Technologies specializes in delivering cutting-edge Learning and Training Content Solutions, alongside Test Preparation Solutions, leveraging state-of-the-art technologies to enhance educational experiences

Founded by Dr. S. M. Nabi, a distinguished figure in the EdTech industry and an alumnus of the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. Dr. Nabi brings extensive experience, having previously served as the General Manager at Wipro Technologies. Innovated series of Ed Tech products (3DM ClassRoom, LearnEngg, SSTOPKLAS since 2011)

The core team comprises highly qualified individuals with Ph.D’s, M.Tech’s, and MSc’s from esteemed institutions such as IIT’s, NIT’s, and other premier government institutes. With over 60 years of collective experience, their expertise lies predominantly in the field of EdTech, demonstrating profound knowledge and skills in leveraging technology for educational purposes

Our EdTech technological service offerings primarily focus on providing Learning and Virtual Lab Solutions tailored for educational institutes, universities, and industrial training purposes.

Our strength lies in our profound expertise in developing comprehensive Learning Solutions tailored for a diverse range of educational levels such as K12, ITI, Polytechnic, B.E, B.Tech, M.Tech, Pharmacy, as well as Skill Development courses.

We specialize in providing tailored solutions for the industrial sector, particularly in Automotive, Home Appliances, Consumer Electronics, and other related industries. Our expertise encompasses both manual development and training content creation, ensuring comprehensive support for our clients in these sectors
