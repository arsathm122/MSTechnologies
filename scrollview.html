Our K–12 LMS is built to amplify teaching and maximise learning.

Edutech's Learning Management System (LMS) makes sure that all students, no matter where they are, have equal access to learning every day. Students can customize their dashboard, check feedback, manage their to-do lists, and sync important dates with their external calendars. Teachers can customize content and assessments to fit each student's learning needs. Quizzes, assessments, and discussions help students work together and get instant feedback. Teachers can communicate directly with students, in groups or with the whole class, and even with parents through messages, audio, and video. Our LMS is designed with smart, easy-to-use features that enhance learning. Plus, it easily connects with other apps and tools, bringing everything together in one central hub.
